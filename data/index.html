Welcome to Sparrow

Sparrow V-AIS is a so called virtual AIS that reports your position, speed and course by transmitting AIS messages to MarineTraffic. The AIS messages are only visibe through the MarineTraffic interface, and not visible to any vessels nearby using regular AIS.

Reporting interval is based on Class B AIS equipment usng SOTDMA protocol (SO). Slower than 2 knots, every 3 minutes. 2 knots or faster, at last every 30 seconds. Please read documentation for more information.
